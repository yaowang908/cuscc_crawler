Chinese renewable resource industry technology innovation strategic alliance (CIAR) and the China-U.S. Chamber of Commerce (CUSCC) announced a strategic partnership\n\n

  	&nbsp;

  	United&nbsp;States&nbsp;Waste&nbsp;Recycling&nbsp;Industries&nbsp;(ISRI)&nbsp;held&nbsp;2011&nbsp;Annual&nbsp;Meeting&nbsp;on&nbsp;April&nbsp;8,&nbsp;2011.&nbsp;During&nbsp;the&nbsp;meeting,&nbsp;Chinese&nbsp;renewable&nbsp;resource&nbsp;industry&nbsp;technology&nbsp;innovation&nbsp;strategic&nbsp;alliance&nbsp;(CIAR)&nbsp;and&nbsp;the&nbsp;China-U.S.&nbsp;Chamber&nbsp;of&nbsp;Commerce&nbsp;(CUSCC)&nbsp;announced&nbsp;a&nbsp;strategic&nbsp;partnership.  

  	
  	The&nbsp;two&nbsp;sides&nbsp;will&nbsp;focus.&nbsp;on&nbsp;energy&nbsp;saving,&nbsp;environmental&nbsp;protection,&nbsp;recycling&nbsp;economy&nbsp;and&nbsp;other&nbsp;areas&nbsp;of&nbsp;mutual&nbsp;interest&nbsp;to&nbsp;carry&nbsp;out&nbsp;in-depth&nbsp;research&nbsp;cooperation.&nbsp;Renewable&nbsp;Resources&nbsp;Industry&nbsp;Alliance&nbsp;Expert&nbsp;Committee,&nbsp;vice&nbsp;chairman&nbsp;and&nbsp;director&nbsp;of&nbsp;Shilong&nbsp;Li&nbsp;and&nbsp;the&nbsp;China-U.S.&nbsp;Chamber&nbsp;of&nbsp;Commerce&nbsp;Wynn&nbsp;Wang&nbsp;signed&nbsp;a&nbsp;cooperation&nbsp;framework&nbsp;agreement&nbsp;in&nbsp;Los&nbsp;Angeles.
