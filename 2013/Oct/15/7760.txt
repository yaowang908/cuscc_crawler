Excelorators, Inc.\n\n

  	Excelorators engages global business professionals and scholars from the premier centers of higher education in the United States to create a forum where current and future leaders share knowledge, experience and insight. We cultivate cross-cultural connections and equip future leaders with the tools, confidence and support they need for success.

  	Excelorator Programs offer three levels of accelerated academic and business enrichment. Each level provides training for those traveling with restricted schedules and language barriers. The lecture series disseminates current information on relevant and exciting topics. Sample industries covered include Economics, Finance, Banking, Communication, Innovation, Technology, Happiness and Leadership.
