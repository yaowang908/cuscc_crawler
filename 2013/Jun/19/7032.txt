William Joseph Sitar\n\n

  	William J. Sitar is CEO, Founder and Broker of Record of Sitar Realty Company, a full-service commercial real estate firm based in New Jersey that provides senior-level advisory services in the areas of office, industrial, retail and land development transactions. Mr. Sitar established the Company in its present form in 1978 on the fundamentals of integrity, professionalism and personalized services.
  	
  	In 2011, Sitar Company was selected to become the New Jersey representative for TCN, a worldwide network of independently owned real estate firms. From 1992 to 2011, Sitar Realty Company was a member of ONCOR International, a global commercial real estate network.
