Tim Yang\n\n

  	Tim Yang, the Vice Chairman of China-U.S. Chamber of Commerce, Executive Director of Auri International Enterprise, was the Vice President of Changkun International Enterprise. Mr. Yang started his business in the U.S., involving in banking, insurance, real estates and natural resource industries. In addition, Mr. Yang is a senior advisor and engineer in the astronautics field.  

  	Mr. Yang was the first western auditing member visiting the Soviet Union&rsquo;s rocket launching site. He represented Lockheed Martin and Hong Kong Asian Satellite Company to take the duty of launching the Great Wall Rocket. Based on his management skills and professional knowledge of astronautics, Mr. Yang made a huge contribution to the growth and development of Lockheed Martin.
