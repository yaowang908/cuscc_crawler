Young Professional Going Global was held in Manhattan\n\n

  	As a promotional partner, China-U.S. Chamber of Commerce has support the Young Professionals Going Global Networking Event which held on May 21th,2015 at the hip Ramscale Studio in West Village. This is an exciting international networking event and been held in conjunction with the 2015 World Trade Week. The objective is to connect and establish relationships between local and international Young Professionals working in New York. Also this event had created great opportunities for young professionals from all over the world.
