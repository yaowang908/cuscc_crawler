China-U.S. Chamber of Commerce Hosts Investment Forum on April 28 in New York City\n\n

  	Leaders in Finance, Alternative Energy, Healthcare and Technology will discuss investment opportunities and trends 

  	&nbsp;

  	New York NY. &ndash; (April 27, 2016)- The China-U.S. Chamber of Commerce (www.cnusa.org)&nbsp; announced that it is hosting the  China Invests in America Forum on Thursday, April 28th in New York City. The event will bring together thought leaders, entrepreneurs and financial experts from China and the United States for a lively discussion about the historic flow of capital into the US from China in 2016. 

  	&nbsp;

  	Direct investment will reach a record $30 billion this year: a 50 percent increase from 2015.&nbsp; The types of investment are expanding from traditional sectors including real estate and raw materials to healthcare, technology and alternative energy, among others.&nbsp; The Forum will feature presentations from experts in these areas including Envision Solar as well as leading financial firms like Merrill Lynch and KKR. 

  	&nbsp;

  	Entrepreneurs and Investors from China will take part to learn about opportunities in the United States. According to Wayne Wang, Chairman of the China-US Chamber of Commerce, &ldquo;There is a growing desire among Chinese business people to invest in America businesses. We believe the Forum will provide them with a good overview of growth areas in different sectors and the networking opportunities during the Forum will enable personal relationships to blossom.&rdquo; 

  	&nbsp;

  	The event is being organized by Heinlyn (www.heinlyn.com), a leading public relations firm in China and the US.&nbsp; To purchase a ticket visit: (https://www.eventbrite.com/e/china-invests-in-america-forum-tickets-23842270874?aff=ebrowse).
