Suning turns to loss; online business revenue climbs 51%\n\n

  	From Chinadaily : Suning Commerce Co Ltd reported a first-half net loss of 749 million yuan ($121.4 million), thefirst interim loss for the appliance retailer in a decade.

  	A&nbsp;year&nbsp;earlier,&nbsp;the&nbsp;company&nbsp;reported&nbsp;a&nbsp;net&nbsp;first-half&nbsp;profit&nbsp;of&nbsp;734&nbsp;million&nbsp;yuan.

  	Its&nbsp;second-quarter&nbsp;net&nbsp;loss&nbsp;narrowed&nbsp;to&nbsp;315&nbsp;million&nbsp;yuan&nbsp;from&nbsp;434&nbsp;million&nbsp;yuan&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;previousquarter.

  	First-half&nbsp;revenue&nbsp;stood&nbsp;at&nbsp;51.1&nbsp;billion&nbsp;yuan,&nbsp;down&nbsp;more&nbsp;than&nbsp;7&nbsp;percent&nbsp;year-on-year.

  	But&nbsp;Suning&#39;s&nbsp;online&nbsp;operations&nbsp;posted&nbsp;a&nbsp;first-half&nbsp;revenue&nbsp;increase&nbsp;of&nbsp;51&nbsp;percent&nbsp;to&nbsp;8.3&nbsp;billionyuan,&nbsp;driven&nbsp;by&nbsp;efforts&nbsp;to&nbsp;upgrade&nbsp;its&nbsp;lineup&nbsp;of&nbsp;products&nbsp;and&nbsp;improve&nbsp;customers&#39;&nbsp;experienceonline

  	The&nbsp;company&nbsp;opened&nbsp;62&nbsp;new&nbsp;stores,&nbsp;of&nbsp;which&nbsp;84&nbsp;percent&nbsp;are&nbsp;in&nbsp;second-&nbsp;and&nbsp;third-tier&nbsp;markets.

  	Meanwhile, 64&nbsp;stores&nbsp;were&nbsp;closed.&nbsp;The&nbsp;retailer&nbsp;operates&nbsp;1,583&nbsp;stores&nbsp;on&nbsp;the&nbsp;Chinese&nbsp;mainland, 27&nbsp;in&nbsp;Hong&nbsp;Kong&nbsp;and&nbsp;13&nbsp;stores&nbsp;in&nbsp;Japan.

  	According&nbsp;to&nbsp;Tian&nbsp;Zheng,&nbsp;a&nbsp;senior&nbsp;researcher&nbsp;at&nbsp;Analysys&nbsp;International,&nbsp;Suning&nbsp;made&nbsp;the&nbsp;rightmove&nbsp;by&nbsp;prioritizing&nbsp;its&nbsp;online&nbsp;sales&nbsp;as&nbsp;its&nbsp;brick-and-mortar&nbsp;stores&nbsp;began&nbsp;to&nbsp;decline.

  	But,&nbsp;he&nbsp;said,&nbsp;it&nbsp;will&nbsp;take&nbsp;years&nbsp;to&nbsp;train&nbsp;consumption&nbsp;habits&nbsp;for&nbsp;Suning&#39;s&nbsp;customers&nbsp;as&nbsp;it&nbsp;tries&nbsp;tocatch&nbsp;up&nbsp;with&nbsp;e-commerce&nbsp;giants&nbsp;Taobao.com&nbsp;and&nbsp;JD.com.

  	The&nbsp;next&nbsp;three&nbsp;years&nbsp;will&nbsp;be&nbsp;vital&nbsp;for&nbsp;Suning&nbsp;as&nbsp;it&nbsp;fights&nbsp;for&nbsp;third&nbsp;place&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;sector.

  	Suning&nbsp;Chairman&nbsp;Zhang&nbsp;Jindong&nbsp;said&nbsp;that&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;Internet&nbsp;era,&nbsp;the&nbsp;days&nbsp;when&nbsp;the&nbsp;market&nbsp;wasdriven&nbsp;by&nbsp;production&nbsp;and&nbsp;sales&nbsp;by&nbsp;sourcing&nbsp;are&nbsp;over.

  	Only&nbsp;by&nbsp;understanding&nbsp;and&nbsp;capturing&nbsp;consumer&nbsp;demand&nbsp;can&nbsp;retailers&nbsp;and&nbsp;suppliers&nbsp;succeed.

  	A&nbsp;previous&nbsp;price&nbsp;war&nbsp;between&nbsp;Suning&nbsp;and&nbsp;JD.com&nbsp;did&nbsp;not&nbsp;kill&nbsp;JD&nbsp;but&nbsp;hurt&nbsp;Suning,&nbsp;prompting&nbsp;thecompany&nbsp;to&nbsp;return&nbsp;to&nbsp;its&nbsp;core&nbsp;value&nbsp;by&nbsp;providing&nbsp;more&nbsp;services&nbsp;than&nbsp;e-commerce&nbsp;players&nbsp;basedon&nbsp;its&nbsp;long-term&nbsp;relationships&nbsp;with&nbsp;suppliers,&nbsp;according&nbsp;to&nbsp;Huang&nbsp;Hongyi,&nbsp;an&nbsp;e-commerce&nbsp;analyst.

  	Its&nbsp;physical&nbsp;stores&nbsp;have&nbsp;offered&nbsp;free&nbsp;protective&nbsp;screens&nbsp;for&nbsp;electronic&nbsp;devices&nbsp;and&nbsp;free&nbsp;trials&nbsp;for&nbsp;itswearable&nbsp;devices&nbsp;to&nbsp;lure&nbsp;more&nbsp;customers,&nbsp;Huang&nbsp;said.

  	Another&nbsp;leading&nbsp;home&nbsp;appliance&nbsp;retailer,&nbsp;Gome&nbsp;Electrical&nbsp;Appliances&nbsp;Holding&nbsp;Ltd,&nbsp;sawcontinued&nbsp;improvement&nbsp;in&nbsp;its&nbsp;operational&nbsp;capabilities&nbsp;and&nbsp;profitability.

  	Its&nbsp;e-commerce&nbsp;business&nbsp;recorded&nbsp;substantial&nbsp;year-on-year&nbsp;growth&nbsp;in&nbsp;revenue.

  	Gome&#39;s&nbsp;revenue&nbsp;increased&nbsp;by&nbsp;more&nbsp;than&nbsp;7&nbsp;percent.

  	Totale-commerce transactions are expected to grow by more than 50 percent as compared withthe corresponding period last year, and consolidated gross profit margin for e-commence isexpected to exceed 7 percent.
