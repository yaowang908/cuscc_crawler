Tencent stepping further into e-finance\n\n

  	From Chinadaily: Tencent&nbsp;Holdings&nbsp;Ltd&nbsp;has&nbsp;launched&nbsp;securities&nbsp;and&nbsp;wealth&nbsp;management&nbsp;services&nbsp;on&nbsp;its&nbsp;live&nbsp;chattool&nbsp;QQ,&nbsp;diving&nbsp;further&nbsp;into&nbsp;Internet&nbsp;finance.

  	The&nbsp;Shenzhen-based&nbsp;company&nbsp;teamed&nbsp;up&nbsp;with&nbsp;five&nbsp;securities&nbsp;firms&nbsp;on&nbsp;Wednesday&nbsp;to&nbsp;offer&nbsp;one-stop&nbsp;financial&nbsp;services&nbsp;online,&nbsp;including&nbsp;setting&nbsp;up&nbsp;securities&nbsp;accounts,&nbsp;customer&nbsp;services&nbsp;andstock&nbsp;exchange&nbsp;services,&nbsp;to&nbsp;its&nbsp;vast&nbsp;base&nbsp;of&nbsp;QQ&nbsp;users.

  	The&nbsp;five&nbsp;companies&nbsp;(Zhongshan&nbsp;Securities&nbsp;Co&nbsp;Ltd,&nbsp;Tongxin&nbsp;Securities&nbsp;Co&nbsp;Ltd,&nbsp;China&nbsp;DragonSecurities&nbsp;Co&nbsp;Ltd,&nbsp;Chinalion&nbsp;Securities&nbsp;Co&nbsp;Ltd&nbsp;and&nbsp;Guangzhou&nbsp;Securities&nbsp;Co&nbsp;Ltd)&nbsp;are&nbsp;the&nbsp;firstgroup&nbsp;of&nbsp;securities&nbsp;firms&nbsp;to&nbsp;set&nbsp;up&nbsp;online&nbsp;finance&nbsp;businesses&nbsp;on&nbsp;QQ.

  	The&nbsp;social&nbsp;networking&nbsp;tool&nbsp;is&nbsp;expected&nbsp;to&nbsp;team&nbsp;up&nbsp;with&nbsp;more&nbsp;securities&nbsp;companies&nbsp;to&nbsp;offer&nbsp;similarservices&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;future.

  	Tan&nbsp;Yi,&nbsp;deputy&nbsp;manager&nbsp;of&nbsp;Tencent&#39;s&nbsp;social&nbsp;networking&nbsp;group,&nbsp;said&nbsp;that&nbsp;the&nbsp;company&nbsp;has&nbsp;a&nbsp;vastnumber&nbsp;of&nbsp;users.&nbsp;The&nbsp;top&nbsp;priority&nbsp;for&nbsp;Tencent,&nbsp;which&nbsp;has&nbsp;built&nbsp;its&nbsp;business&nbsp;empire&nbsp;on&nbsp;all&nbsp;kinds&nbsp;ofsocial&nbsp;tools,&nbsp;such&nbsp;as&nbsp;QQ&nbsp;and&nbsp;WeChat,&nbsp;is&nbsp;to&nbsp;offer&nbsp;more&nbsp;and&nbsp;better&nbsp;services&nbsp;to&nbsp;its&nbsp;users.

  	&quot;On&nbsp;the&nbsp;one&nbsp;hand,&nbsp;we&nbsp;need&nbsp;to&nbsp;identify&nbsp;the&nbsp;services&nbsp;that&nbsp;can&nbsp;meet&nbsp;the&nbsp;demand&nbsp;of&nbsp;our&nbsp;individualusers.&nbsp;On&nbsp;the&nbsp;other&nbsp;hand,&nbsp;we&nbsp;need&nbsp;to&nbsp;help&nbsp;enterprises&nbsp;connect&nbsp;with&nbsp;end-users,&nbsp;matching&nbsp;theirservices&nbsp;with&nbsp;our&nbsp;users,&quot;&nbsp;he&nbsp;told&nbsp;a&nbsp;news&nbsp;conference&nbsp;in&nbsp;Shenzhen&nbsp;on&nbsp;Wednesday.

  	On&nbsp;the&nbsp;same&nbsp;day,&nbsp;Shenzhen&nbsp;Kingdom&nbsp;Technology&nbsp;Co&nbsp;Ltd,&nbsp;a&nbsp;financial&nbsp;software&nbsp;and&nbsp;systemintegration&nbsp;producer,&nbsp;announced&nbsp;a&nbsp;partnership&nbsp;with&nbsp;Tencent&nbsp;covering&nbsp;the&nbsp;marketing&nbsp;and&nbsp;sales&nbsp;of&nbsp;QQ.

  	Kingdom&nbsp;Technology&nbsp;will&nbsp;help&nbsp;QQ&nbsp;build&nbsp;the&nbsp;financial&nbsp;service&nbsp;platform&nbsp;to&nbsp;directly&nbsp;link&nbsp;moresecurities&nbsp;companies&nbsp;with&nbsp;QQ&nbsp;users.

  	The&nbsp;move&nbsp;is&nbsp;seen&nbsp;as&nbsp;Tencent&#39;s&nbsp;latest&nbsp;attempt&nbsp;to&nbsp;push&nbsp;further&nbsp;into&nbsp;China&#39;s&nbsp;emerging&nbsp;Internetfinance&nbsp;sector.&nbsp;Tencent&#39;s&nbsp;rival&nbsp;Alibaba&nbsp;Group&nbsp;Holding&nbsp;Ltd&nbsp;was&nbsp;a&nbsp;pioneer&nbsp;in&nbsp;this&nbsp;sector&nbsp;in&nbsp;China&nbsp;bylaunching&nbsp;Yu&#39;ebao&nbsp;last&nbsp;summer.

  	Yu&#39;ebao,&nbsp;designed&nbsp;to&nbsp;make&nbsp;it&nbsp;easy&nbsp;for&nbsp;Alibaba&#39;s&nbsp;Alipay&nbsp;users&nbsp;to&nbsp;invest&nbsp;their&nbsp;idle&nbsp;cash&nbsp;and&nbsp;get&nbsp;ahigher&nbsp;interest&nbsp;rate&nbsp;than&nbsp;typical&nbsp;bank&nbsp;savings&nbsp;deposits,&nbsp;has&nbsp;gained&nbsp;great&nbsp;popularity&nbsp;in&nbsp;China,especially&nbsp;among&nbsp;young&nbsp;people.

  	Many&nbsp;Internet&nbsp;companies&nbsp;have&nbsp;followed&nbsp;Yu&#39;ebao&#39;s&nbsp;lead&nbsp;by&nbsp;launching&nbsp;wealth&nbsp;managementservices.

  	&quot;Through&nbsp;teaming&nbsp;up&nbsp;with&nbsp;technology&nbsp;solutions&nbsp;provider&nbsp;Kingdom,&nbsp;Tencent&nbsp;certainly&nbsp;has&nbsp;seriousplans&nbsp;about&nbsp;Internet&nbsp;finance,&quot;&nbsp;said&nbsp;Qian&nbsp;Haili,&nbsp;an&nbsp;Internet&nbsp;finance&nbsp;analyst&nbsp;with&nbsp;the&nbsp;China&nbsp;E-Commerce&nbsp;Research&nbsp;Center.

  	She&nbsp;said&nbsp;that&nbsp;Tencent&#39;s&nbsp;agreement&nbsp;with&nbsp;Shenzhen&nbsp;Kingdom&nbsp;can&nbsp;be&nbsp;seen&nbsp;as&nbsp;a&nbsp;counterattackagainst&nbsp;Alibaba,&nbsp;whose&nbsp;founder&nbsp;Jack&nbsp;Ma&nbsp;in&nbsp;April&nbsp;announced&nbsp;a&nbsp;plan&nbsp;to&nbsp;pay&nbsp;3.3&nbsp;billion&nbsp;yuan($531.78&nbsp;million)&nbsp;to&nbsp;take&nbsp;a&nbsp;controlling&nbsp;stake&nbsp;in&nbsp;financial&nbsp;software&nbsp;firm&nbsp;Hundsun&nbsp;Technologies&nbsp;Inc.

  	&quot;Both&nbsp;of&nbsp;these&nbsp;two&nbsp;companies&nbsp;have&nbsp;great&nbsp;ambitions&nbsp;in&nbsp;Internet&nbsp;finance.&nbsp;And&nbsp;QQ,&nbsp;which&nbsp;has&nbsp;amonthly&nbsp;active&nbsp;user&nbsp;population&nbsp;of&nbsp;more&nbsp;than&nbsp;800&nbsp;million,&nbsp;can&nbsp;serve&nbsp;as&nbsp;a&nbsp;great&nbsp;base&nbsp;to&nbsp;promoteInternet&nbsp;finance,&quot;&nbsp;said&nbsp;Qian.

  	&quot;However,&nbsp;since&nbsp;people&nbsp;are&nbsp;used&nbsp;to&nbsp;having&nbsp;fun&nbsp;on&nbsp;QQ&nbsp;by&nbsp;chatting&nbsp;with&nbsp;their&nbsp;friends,&nbsp;many&nbsp;mayfind&nbsp;it&nbsp;difficult&nbsp;to&nbsp;treat&nbsp;it&nbsp;as&nbsp;a&nbsp;serious&nbsp;platform&nbsp;for&nbsp;wealth&nbsp;management,&quot;&nbsp;she&nbsp;said.

  	Report:online&nbsp;finance&nbsp;to&nbsp;be&nbsp;reined&nbsp;in

  	As&nbsp;China&nbsp;perfects&nbsp;its&nbsp;regulatory&nbsp;policies,&nbsp;the&nbsp;Chinese&nbsp;Internet&nbsp;finance&nbsp;sector&nbsp;will&nbsp;slowly&nbsp;grow&nbsp;tobecome&nbsp;a&nbsp;supplementary&nbsp;part&nbsp;of&nbsp;the&nbsp;traditional&nbsp;finance&nbsp;sector,&nbsp;according&nbsp;to&nbsp;a&nbsp;report&nbsp;released&nbsp;byBOCOM&nbsp;International&nbsp;Holdings&nbsp;Co&nbsp;Ltd&nbsp;on&nbsp;Thursday.

  	It&nbsp;quoted&nbsp;Zhang&nbsp;Xiaopu,&nbsp;a&nbsp;policy&nbsp;research&nbsp;official&nbsp;at&nbsp;the&nbsp;China&nbsp;Banking&nbsp;Regulatory&nbsp;Commission,as&nbsp;saying&nbsp;that&nbsp;even&nbsp;as&nbsp;China&#39;s&nbsp;Internet&nbsp;finance&nbsp;sector&nbsp;attracts&nbsp;more&nbsp;and&nbsp;more&nbsp;participants,&nbsp;it&nbsp;maytrigger&nbsp;systemic&nbsp;risks,&nbsp;necessitating&nbsp;a&nbsp;higher&nbsp;level&nbsp;of&nbsp;regulation.

  	Yang&nbsp;Qingli,&nbsp;managing&nbsp;director&nbsp;of&nbsp;BOCOM,&nbsp;said&nbsp;that&nbsp;the&nbsp;rapid&nbsp;development&nbsp;of&nbsp;the&nbsp;sectorstemmed&nbsp;from&nbsp;the&nbsp;regulatory&nbsp;differences&nbsp;between&nbsp;the&nbsp;traditional&nbsp;finance&nbsp;sector&nbsp;and&nbsp;that&nbsp;online,along&nbsp;with&nbsp;China&#39;s&nbsp;low&nbsp;interest&nbsp;rate&nbsp;marketization.

  	When&nbsp;the&nbsp;sector&nbsp;matures,&nbsp;Internet&nbsp;finance&nbsp;will&nbsp;supplement&nbsp;the&nbsp;traditional&nbsp;finance&nbsp;sector,&nbsp;andinnovative&nbsp;commercial&nbsp;banks&nbsp;will&nbsp;stand&nbsp;out,&nbsp;Yang&nbsp;said.

  	&quot;The&nbsp;Internet&nbsp;serves&nbsp;as&nbsp;a&nbsp;channel,&nbsp;and&nbsp;the&nbsp;nature&nbsp;of&nbsp;Internet&nbsp;finance&nbsp;is&nbsp;finance,&quot;&nbsp;he&nbsp;said. &quot;Theadvantage&nbsp;of&nbsp;the&nbsp;channel&nbsp;is&nbsp;that&nbsp;at&nbsp;the&nbsp;beginning,&nbsp;it&nbsp;attracts&nbsp;clients,&nbsp;and&nbsp;risk&nbsp;pricing&nbsp;can&nbsp;be&nbsp;moreimportant&nbsp;then,&nbsp;especially&nbsp;as&nbsp;China&nbsp;promotes&nbsp;the&nbsp;interest&nbsp;rate&nbsp;reforms.&quot;

  	Internet&nbsp;finance&nbsp;includes&nbsp;online&nbsp;third-party&nbsp;payment,&nbsp;peer-to-peer&nbsp;lending,&nbsp;crowdfunding&nbsp;andonline&nbsp;wealth&nbsp;management.

  	Regulating&nbsp;Internet&nbsp;finance&nbsp;is&nbsp;mainly&nbsp;related&nbsp;to&nbsp;the&nbsp;online&nbsp;third-party&nbsp;payments&nbsp;and&nbsp;is&nbsp;looser&nbsp;inChina&nbsp;than&nbsp;in&nbsp;Europe&nbsp;and&nbsp;the&nbsp;United&nbsp;States,&nbsp;the&nbsp;BOCOM&nbsp;report&nbsp;said.

  	The&nbsp;trading&nbsp;volume&nbsp;of&nbsp;online&nbsp;third-party&nbsp;payments&nbsp;totaled&nbsp;6&nbsp;trillion&nbsp;yuan&nbsp;($960.6&nbsp;billion)&nbsp;in&nbsp;2013,while&nbsp;in&nbsp;2008,&nbsp;it&nbsp;stood&nbsp;at&nbsp;only&nbsp;260&nbsp;billion&nbsp;yuan,&nbsp;according&nbsp;to&nbsp;iResearch,&nbsp;a&nbsp;China-based&nbsp;Internetconsulting&nbsp;group.

  	The&nbsp;asset&nbsp;scale&nbsp;of&nbsp;Yu&#39;ebao,&nbsp;an&nbsp;online&nbsp;financial&nbsp;product&nbsp;launched&nbsp;by&nbsp;Internet&nbsp;giant&nbsp;Alibaba&nbsp;thatfocuses&nbsp;on&nbsp;money&nbsp;market&nbsp;funds,&nbsp;totaled&nbsp;500&nbsp;billion&nbsp;yuan&nbsp;by&nbsp;Feb&nbsp;1&nbsp;of&nbsp;this&nbsp;year,&nbsp;up&nbsp;from&nbsp;55.7billion&nbsp;yuan&nbsp;on&nbsp;Sept&nbsp;1, 2013,&nbsp;according&nbsp;to&nbsp;Wind&nbsp;Information&nbsp;Co&nbsp;Ltd.
