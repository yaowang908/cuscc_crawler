Toyota Supplier Sees China Sales Doubling on Orders From VW, GM\n\n

  	&nbsp;

  	&nbsp; Republished&nbsp;from&nbsp;Bloomberg&nbsp;News,By&nbsp;Masatsugu&nbsp;Horie&nbsp;and&nbsp;Anna&nbsp;Mukai,Jan&nbsp;28,&nbsp;2014   &nbsp;  

  	Tsubakimoto&nbsp;Chain&nbsp;Co.&nbsp;(6371),&nbsp;a&nbsp;Toyota&nbsp;Motor&nbsp;Corp.&nbsp;(7203)&nbsp;supplier,&nbsp;forecasts&nbsp;China&nbsp;auto&nbsp;parts&nbsp;salesto&nbsp;more&nbsp;than&nbsp;double&nbsp; in&nbsp;four&nbsp;years&nbsp;as&nbsp;carmakers&nbsp;including&nbsp;Volkswagen&nbsp;AG&nbsp;and&nbsp;General&nbsp;Motors&nbsp;Co.&nbsp;raise&nbsp;orders&nbsp;to&nbsp;diversify&nbsp;supply&nbsp;chains.   

  	The&nbsp;maker&nbsp;of&nbsp;transmission&nbsp;chains&nbsp;and&nbsp;gears&nbsp;expects&nbsp;sales&nbsp;to&nbsp;jump&nbsp;to&nbsp;more&nbsp;than&nbsp;12&nbsp;billion&nbsp;yen&nbsp;($117&nbsp;million)&nbsp;by&nbsp;March&nbsp;2018,&nbsp;Toru&nbsp;Fujiwara,&nbsp;managing&nbsp;executive&nbsp;officer,&nbsp;said&nbsp;in&nbsp;a&nbsp;Jan.&nbsp;27&nbsp;interview.&nbsp;The&nbsp;board&nbsp;has&nbsp;approved&nbsp;plans&nbsp;for&nbsp;a&nbsp;new&nbsp;plant&nbsp;in&nbsp; China&nbsp;as&nbsp;early&nbsp;as&nbsp;this&nbsp;year,&nbsp;he&nbsp;said.   

  	Tsubakimoto&nbsp;Chain&nbsp;is&nbsp;planning&nbsp;to&nbsp;expand&nbsp;in&nbsp;China,&nbsp;where&nbsp;its&nbsp;auto&nbsp;parts&nbsp;sales&nbsp;are&nbsp;projected&nbsp;to&nbsp;reach&nbsp;6&nbsp;billion&nbsp;yen&nbsp;this&nbsp;fiscal&nbsp;year&nbsp;fueled&nbsp;by&nbsp;the&nbsp;race&nbsp;between&nbsp;GM&nbsp;and&nbsp;VW&nbsp;to&nbsp;become&nbsp;China&rsquo;s&nbsp;biggest&nbsp;carmaker.&nbsp;As&nbsp;the&nbsp;Germany&nbsp;and&nbsp;U.S.-based&nbsp;carmakersvie&nbsp;for&nbsp;the&nbsp;No.&nbsp;1&nbsp;spot&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;world&rsquo;s&nbsp;biggest&nbsp;auto&nbsp;market,&nbsp;they&nbsp;are&nbsp;diversifying&nbsp;orders&nbsp;to&nbsp;reduce&nbsp;the&nbsp;risk&nbsp;of&nbsp;having&nbsp;just&nbsp;one&nbsp; supplier&nbsp;for&nbsp;each&nbsp;part,&nbsp;Fujiwara&nbsp;said.   &quot;That&nbsp;would&nbsp;be&nbsp;a&nbsp;tailwind&nbsp;for&nbsp;us,&quot;&nbsp;Fujiwara&nbsp;said&nbsp;in&nbsp;Osaka,&nbsp;where&nbsp;the&nbsp;company&nbsp;is&nbsp;based.  

  	Tsubakimoto&nbsp;Chain,&nbsp;which&nbsp;also&nbsp;makes&nbsp;conveyor&nbsp;belts,&nbsp;jumped&nbsp;74&nbsp;percent&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;past&nbsp;12&nbsp;months&nbsp;in&nbsp;Tokyotrading,&nbsp;compared&nbsp;with&nbsp;a&nbsp;38&nbsp;percent&nbsp;gain&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;Nikkei&nbsp;225&nbsp;Stock&nbsp;Average.   

  	Net&nbsp;income&nbsp;will&nbsp;probably&nbsp;jump&nbsp;25&nbsp;percent&nbsp;to&nbsp;9.3&nbsp;billion&nbsp;yen&nbsp;for&nbsp;the&nbsp;year&nbsp;ending&nbsp;in&nbsp;March,&nbsp;according&nbsp;to&nbsp;the&nbsp;average&nbsp;of&nbsp;seven&nbsp;analysts&rsquo;&nbsp;estimates&nbsp;compiled&nbsp;by&nbsp;Bloomberg.&nbsp;Sales&nbsp;may&nbsp;climb&nbsp;15&nbsp;percent&nbsp;to&nbsp;173&nbsp;billion&nbsp;yen,&nbsp;the&nbsp;estimates&nbsp;show.   

  	Carmakers&nbsp;Boom   

  	VW&nbsp;has&nbsp;said&nbsp;it&nbsp;will&nbsp;add&nbsp;seven&nbsp;car&nbsp;plants&nbsp;in&nbsp;China&nbsp;and&nbsp;increase&nbsp;production&nbsp;capacity&nbsp;there&nbsp;to&nbsp;4&nbsp;million&nbsp;vehicles&nbsp;a&nbsp;year&nbsp;by&nbsp;2018.&nbsp;GM&nbsp;plans&nbsp;to&nbsp;invest&nbsp;$11&nbsp;billion&nbsp;in&nbsp;China&nbsp;by&nbsp;2016&nbsp;and&nbsp;add&nbsp;four&nbsp;plants&nbsp;that&nbsp;will&nbsp;boost&nbsp;annual&nbsp;capacity&nbsp;to&nbsp;about&nbsp;5&nbsp;million&nbsp;units.   

  	&quot;As&nbsp;a&nbsp;global&nbsp;operating&nbsp;company&nbsp;we&nbsp;work&nbsp;together&nbsp;with&nbsp;global&nbsp;operating&nbsp;suppliers,&rdquo;&nbsp;Larissa&nbsp;Braun,&nbsp;a&nbsp;spokeswoman&nbsp;with&nbsp;VW&nbsp;Group&nbsp;China&nbsp;in&nbsp;Beijing,&nbsp;said&nbsp;in&nbsp;e-mailed&nbsp;comments.&nbsp;&ldquo;To&nbsp;secure&nbsp;globally&nbsp;stable&nbsp;and&nbsp;efficient&nbsp;supplier&nbsp;chains&nbsp;we&nbsp;cooperate&nbsp;withthe&nbsp;most&nbsp;competitive&nbsp;and&nbsp;cost&nbsp;efficient&nbsp;supplier&nbsp;mark-ets&nbsp;to&nbsp;improve&nbsp;continuously&nbsp;our&nbsp;costs.&rdquo;   

  	Dayna&nbsp;Hart,&nbsp;a&nbsp;spokeswoman&nbsp;with&nbsp;GM&nbsp;in&nbsp;Shanghai,&nbsp;didn&rsquo;t&nbsp;immediately&nbsp;respond&nbsp;to&nbsp;an&nbsp;e-mail&nbsp;sent&nbsp;after&nbsp;office&nbsp;hours&nbsp;in&nbsp;China.   

  	Orders&nbsp;from&nbsp;Japanese&nbsp;automakers&nbsp;in&nbsp;China&nbsp;led&nbsp;by&nbsp;Toyota&nbsp;are&nbsp;also&nbsp;expected&nbsp;to&nbsp;rise,&nbsp;Fujiwara&nbsp;said.&nbsp;Demand&nbsp;for&nbsp;Japanese&nbsp; vehicles&nbsp;in&nbsp;China&nbsp;has&nbsp;recovered&nbsp;from&nbsp;a&nbsp;consumer&nbsp;backlash&nbsp;stemming&nbsp;from&nbsp;a&nbsp;territorial&nbsp;dispute&nbsp;between&nbsp;the&nbsp;two&nbsp;countries&nbsp; over&nbsp;a&nbsp;group&nbsp;of&nbsp;islands.   

  	Fujiwara&nbsp;said&nbsp;U.S.&nbsp;sales&nbsp;of&nbsp;auto&nbsp;parts&nbsp;will&nbsp;probably&nbsp;reach&nbsp;17.5&nbsp;billion&nbsp;yen&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;year&nbsp;ending&nbsp;March2014,&nbsp;then&nbsp;expand&nbsp;about&nbsp; 15&nbsp;percent&nbsp;by&nbsp;March&nbsp;2017.&nbsp;The&nbsp;company&nbsp;may&nbsp;have&nbsp;to&nbsp;consider&nbsp;adding&nbsp;capacity&nbsp;at&nbsp;its&nbsp;plant&nbsp;in&nbsp;Tennessee&nbsp;or&nbsp;building&nbsp;a&nbsp;new&nbsp;factory&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;next&nbsp;three&nbsp;years,&nbsp;he&nbsp;said.   

  	North&nbsp;America&nbsp;accounted&nbsp;for&nbsp;about&nbsp;17&nbsp;percent&nbsp;of&nbsp;Tsubakimoto&nbsp;Chain&rsquo;s&nbsp;total&nbsp;revenue&nbsp;last&nbsp;fiscal&nbsp;year,according&nbsp;to&nbsp;data&nbsp; compiled&nbsp;by&nbsp;Bloomberg.
